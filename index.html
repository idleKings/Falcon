Ether Falcon Farm is the #1 falcon farming simulator and idle game on the blockchain. The more falcon you have, the more eggs they lay (each falcon lays at a rate of 1 per day). Hatch more falcon with your eggs to multiply your production, or cash them out for Ethereum!

Falcons Are Frail Technology

To prevent inflation, Falcon Farms have several tricks over their competitors. Falcons are attached to their progeny and one third of your nest will die if you sell your eggs. Those willing to make the greatest sacrifice can become the Snake, and collect tribute on every egg sale.
